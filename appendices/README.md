## Contents

| Item | What's Inside |
|------|--------------|

## When to Use These

- **Glossary** — You hit a term you don't recognize, or you want a quick refresher on a concept before diving into a chapter.
- **Resources** — You've finished a chapter and want to go deeper, or you need the original paper/source for something the guide references.
- **Cheat Sheet** — You're in the middle of building and need a quick lookup: which model to use, what the SDK call looks like, how much it costs.
